About Us

Happily Ever After is the UK’s most trusted source for wedding planning and inspiration. We’ll help you stay on top the latest trends in order to help plan your dream wedding. Because after all, we all deserve a Happily Ever After.

Gabriella, Betsy, Merissa & Hayley, co-founded Happily Ever After with one mission in mind – to make wedding planning easy no matter the size. This site is designed with you and your big day in mind, taking the stress out of the planning and adding organisation. Think of it as our gift to you.

  • Discover your style. We have thousands of photo inspiration, from flowers, to venues, to wedding cakes and stunning venues.

  • Get the finest cuisine With our state of the art food directory, there is 100% chance we have your dream delicacy, with our range covering, Italian, African, Caribbean and many many more.
  • Answer any of your questions!
