About Us

Happily Ever After is the most trusted source for wedding planning and inspiration in the UK. We will help you stay on top the latest trends in order to help plan your dream wedding. Because after all, we all deserve a Happily Ever After.

Gabriella, Betsy, Merissa & Hayley, co-founded Happily Ever After with one mission in mind; to make wedding planning easy no matter the size. This site is designed with you and your big day in mind, taking the stress out of the planning and adding organisation.

Think of it as our gift to you.
